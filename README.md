
```text
Strength - The Savage
```

Core Talent - Barbarian

_"Surprised anybody'd want to tangle with you. Heck, you could go deathclaw hunting with a switch."_

Twice per session choose one of the following:
  - Push yourself for another talent without receiving a mark.
  - Gain bonus dice for a roll for every harm and two marks you have.
  - Gain potency on any roll related to strength based actions.

Growth - Every 2 levels, you can use Barbarian one more time per session.

```text
Perception - The Marksman
```

Core Talent - Sharpshooter

_"Nothing gets by you, huh? Coulda used you when I lost my keys last month."_

Twice per session choose one of the following:
  - Gain potency on a roll requiring accuracy at distance.
  - Squint and get a Reveal no matter the range, and see all details.
  - Get a guaranteed Reveal about your surroundings.
  
Growth - Every 2 levels, you can use Sharpshooter one more time per session.

```text
Endurance - The Bulwark
```

Core Talent - Toughness

_"I guess that explains how you're still alive. You're built solid as an oak."_

Twice per session choose one of the following:
  - Shrug off a new or old mark like it was nothing.
  - Ignore all thorns from harms, marks, and conditions for a roll.
  - Take a mark, harm, or condition, meant for someone else.
  
Growth - Every 2 levels, you can use Toughness one more time per session.

```text
Charisma - The Face
```

Core Talent - Smooth Talker

_"Good to see them bullets didn't affect your charm none."_

Twice per session choose one of the following:
  - Convince someone of an at least partially believable lie with ease.
  - Get a perfect Reveal on the motivation and emotions of someone.
  - Persuade someone to put a price on something or lower their price.
  
Growth - Every 2 levels, you can use Smooth Talker one more time per session.

```text
Intelligence - The Labcoat
```

Core Talent - Science!

_"Look at that. Maybe them bullets done your brain some good."_

Twice per session choose one of the following:
  - Instantly understand how something functions and what it can do.
  - Make a prediction about the future and get a Hint or a Reveal.
  - Learn what impact a theoretical action roll would provoke beforehand.
  
Growth - Every 2 levels, you can use Science! one more time per session.

```text
Agility - The Operative
```

Core Talent - Infiltrator

_"Most patients don't get out of bed after being shot and then move like they was in perfect control. You're unusual, I'll say that."_

Twice per session choose one of the following:
  - Remain completely undetected for a scene within reason.
  - Steal something from someone without being noticed.
  - Gain potency on any roll related to agility based actions.
  
Growth - Every 2 levels, you can use Infiltrator one more time per session.

```text
Luck - The Stranger
```

Core Talent - Stacked Deck

_"With Luck like yours I'm surprised them bullets didn't just turn right around and climb back into the gun."_

Twice per session choose one of the following:
  - Upgrade the success level of a roll you've made by one tier.
  - Reroll some or all dice that rolled a 1 during someone else's action roll.
  - Reduce the amount of thorns on a roll by one.
  
Growth - Every 2 levels, you can use Stack Deck one more time per session.
